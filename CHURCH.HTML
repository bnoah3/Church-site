Our Story

A Ministry Born
from Faith & Love

International Jesus Light was founded on a simple yet powerful conviction: that every soul deserves to encounter God's unconditional love. Under the faithful leadership of the founding pastors, what began as a single congregation in Arusha has grown into a vibrant ministry spanning four churches and two children's homes.

Our calling extends far beyond Sunday worship. Moved by the needs of the most vulnerable — children without parents, families without support — we established our orphanage ministry to offer shelter, education, spiritual formation, and above all, a sense of belonging. Every child in our care is treated as a gift from God and raised as a member of our extended family.
